      About Smart Route Finder

      Revolutionizing Navigation across Pakistan

      Our Mission & Vision

      Smart Route Finder Pakistan is designed to bridge the gap between people and places. Our mission is to provide an efficient, fast, and highly reliable navigation tool tailored specifically for Pakistan's diverse landscape. We envision a connected country where travel planning is seamless and accessible to everyone.

      Development Journey

      What started as an ambitious project has grown into a robust navigation application. The journey began with a simple idea: mapping a few major cities using basic graph algorithms. Through rigorous testing, feedback, and countless iterations, we integrated real-world road geometries via the OSRM API and expanded our network to cover over 25 cities. Every challenge faced along the way has refined our routing engine to ensure precision and reliability.

      How It Works

      A step-by-step walkthrough of the route finding process

      1

      Cities as Graph Nodes

      Each Pakistani city is represented as a node in a weighted graph data structure, with its real geographic coordinates.

      2

      Roads as Weighted Edges

      Road connections between cities become edges, with approximate driving distances (in km) as weights.

      3

      Dijkstra Finds Shortest Path

      The algorithm explores all possible paths from your starting city, always expanding the shortest known distance first, until it reaches the destination.

      4

      OSRM Draws Real Roads

      The route waypoints are sent to the OSRM API, which returns the actual road geometry — not just straight lines, but real highways and turns.

      5

      Real Driving Duration

      OSRM also returns the estimated driving time based on road types and speed limits, giving you accurate travel time estimates.
